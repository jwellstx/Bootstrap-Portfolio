About Me

My Name is Justin Wells and I grew up in Colleyville, Texas, born in Irving, Texas. After graduating from Grapevine High School in 2007, I attended college at the University of Texas at Austin. I graduated college in 2011 with a bachelors in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

In 2009, I began my internship at Intel Corp, joining full time in Fall of 2011 when I Graduated. Since then I have been working at Intel for almost 10 years as a Product Development Engineer. My work primary includes debug and validation both pre and post silicon to find and fix defects. My primary role is desinging automation tools in Python.

My Hobbies include riding motorcycles, playing videogames, playing with my horses and Boxer dog, a little woodworking and having a cold beer with friends on a nice day.
